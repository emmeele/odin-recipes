Labneh

What is labneh?

Labneh is basically: a very thick, creamy, Middle Eastern style yogurt cheese. It has the consistency of American cream cheese, but with a little more flavor, complexity and tang. And of the best part? It has all the healthy probiotics of cultured yogurt. In a nutshell, labneh is creamy dreamy heavenly goodness! The perfect canvas for all your creative toppings. And I can’t wait to see what you do with this!

Let's make this healthy delicacy!

Ingredients

  • Full fat whole milk yoghurt
  • Salt
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Place a kitchen towel or cheesecloth ( double layer) over a strainer, over a bowl.

  2. Salt the yogurt (full fat, plain) and pour it in the towel set over the strainer.

  3. Strain the yogurt over the sink.

  4. After 24 hours, remove the whey – and then decide if your labneh is thick enough for your liking. Usually, 24 hours is perfect for a “dip”, but sometimes for a thicker, cheese-like version, I’ll let it strain for an additional 24 hours. Up to you.
